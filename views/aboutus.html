Some eat to live, while others live to eat. Everyone here at SRM, day scholars or hosteliers, come under the latter category.

While searching for good places to eat, we have a set of criteria in mind. Great taste, affordable prices and within our reach. But it isn't always possible to have all three fulfilled. That becomes frustrating. So we, developed a system that could help you find your match and treat your taste buds well.

We developed, SRM Eats. A food recommendation system, where we give you the list of best places, with the tastiest food, based on the recommendations of your fellow mates.

Up until now, we have worked on the following things:

  1. The team has worked hard on making the form that covers all the cuisines, restaurants in and around SRM and having people to fill them.
  2. EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis) has been performed on the datasets so far collected.
  3. A prototype of the recommendation engine is being deployed on Spark.
  4. The home page of the website is ready to be presented.

AIMs

  • Collecting more data
  • Improving the prototype
  • Deploying the model
  • improving the website
  • Final app deployment
